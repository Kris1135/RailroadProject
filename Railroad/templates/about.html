About

Railroad project for Professor Peter Barnett's database course, the topic assigned was an alteration of task 5 which resulted in this application implementing an extension of the Railroad to the the south with trains starting and ending in Roanoake, Richmond and Lynchburg, VA. We were allowed to make them skip stops as long as their schedule fits around the ones running in between DC and Boston.

Code can be found at: https://github.com/Kris1135/RailroadProject
